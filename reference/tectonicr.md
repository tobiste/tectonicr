# library(tectonicr)

Modeling the Direction of the Maximum Horizontal Stress using Relative
Plate Motion

## Details

Further details and theoretical background are provided by Wdowinski
(1998) and Stephan et al. (2023).

## Note

A list of documented functions may be viewed by typing
[`help(package="tectonicr")`](https://tobiste.github.io/tectonicr/reference).

## References

Wdowinski (1998) "A theory of intraplate tectonics". *JGR: Solid Earth*,
103(3), 5037\<U+2013\>5059.

Stephan, T., Enkelmann, E., and Kroner, U. "Analyzing the horizontal
orientation of the crustal stress adjacent to plate boundaries". *Sci
Rep* 13. 15590 (2023).
